# 1.16-pre4

We've now released Minecraft 1.16 Pre-release 4, with tweaked spawn rates and another round of bug fixes.

## Changes in 1.16 Pre-Release 4

-   Tweaked hostile spawn rates in Warped Forests and Soulsand Valleys
